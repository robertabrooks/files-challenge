Walter White

Walter White plays the role of an extremely overqualified highschool chemistry teacher who gets diagnosed with cancer. After his diagnoses, he gets fired from his job and in order to support his family he starts cooking meth, "blue meth" in particular, with his partner Jesse Pinkman. Walter slowly begins to separate from his wife but still tries to keep in touch with his kids. Walter spirals into a life of crime, lying both to his family and friends, and chaos unravels as the drug world starts to consume him. Walter proves to everyone around him that he is the most powerful meth cooker and distributor which all started in the city of Albuquerque, New Mexico.

Bryan Cranston plays the role of Walter White in the infamous tv series Breaking Bad!
